[Gameplay]
	[Controls]
		Z - Press to fire bullets. Press and Hold for bullet barrage.
		X - Press to activate bomb. It takes 1.0 power level and shoots a large beam that clear the screen of bullets and deals a ton of damage, as well as making the player invulnerable for a 5 seconds.
		SHIFT - Press and hold for focus mode. It slows down the player's movement and to show the player's hit box.
		ARROW KEYS - Move the player top, down, left, right, respective of it's arrow key.
